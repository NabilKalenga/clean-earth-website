Understanding Pollution

A brief overview of pollution and its effects on people and the environment.

Air Pollution

Causes

Air pollution is caused by emissions from vehicles, factories, and burning fossil fuels.

Effects

Air pollution harms human health and contributes to global environmental problems.

Water Pollution

Causes

Water pollution happens when waste and chemicals enter rivers, lakes, and oceans.

Effects

It harms aquatic life and makes water unsafe for humans.

Soil Pollution

Causes

Soil pollution comes from chemicals, pesticides, and improper waste disposal.

Effects

It damages crops, soil organisms, and ecosystems.

Summary: Reducing pollution requires cleaner energy and better waste management.
